gentle currents - a collection by FINØ

gentle currents is a collection of moments of finding myself in loud silence. chasing my own shadows, different experiences of being intrigued by foliage and clouds and the internal serenity within. yeah, corny but i stay truthful, an admiration for the blooms and the stillness of moving winds. and i’ve always enjoyed the presence of nature, and as someone creative it is only a takeaway on reminding yourself whatcha know and don’t know. observe the bugs. pick the grass. run away into the mountains and hills. because once you go you don’t come back and that’s for the good of your mind.
